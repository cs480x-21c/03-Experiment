This survey is a project for the Worcester Polytechnic Institute course

Data Visualization. This project was inspired by Cleveland and McGill's

1984 paper. Where they researched people's perception on different types

of charts.

 By choosing to take this survey you will remain anonymous.

You may stop this experiment at any time by closing this tab or window.

Results will only be recorded when you finnish the survey.

 In this experiment, you are asked to judge

What is the percent of a smaller value to a larger value in several charts.

We recommend you make quick judgments of each chart.

This experiment will take roughly 15min.
