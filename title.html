The following is an experiment conducted by Yonatan Weiner, Parker Coady, and Errica Cheng for Project 3 of CS480X.

On each page will be a procedurally-generated graph. Your task is to guess the percentage the smaller datapoint is to the larger datapoint. For instance, if you think the smaller marked datapoint is about 25% of the larger one, simply type 25 and submit it.

There will be 20 bar graphs, 20 Sankey graphs, and 20 bubble charts.

At the end of the experiment, please download the CSV file and send it to one of us.

By continuing with this experiment, you agree for your submitted data to be used in this project. No personal identifying information will be collected.

To continue, press "Submit Guess".
